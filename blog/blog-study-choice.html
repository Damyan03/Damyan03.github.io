My study choice

The reason I made my study choice for IT is because I could actually see the impact it has on our daily lives. I want to learn how to program so I can use my software to increase the quality of life. I find programming to be fun because of the endless possibilities you have when creating.

I want to establish myself as a programmer and I believe that this programme will help me realise my ambition. I want to become someone who is capable of doing all sorts of stuff in different languages and I want people to seek me for help when they need it. I am excited for the practical-based studying where I can put my skills to the test. I think it's going to be a challenging but fun experience and I can't wait to be a part of it.
