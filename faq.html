FAQ

Q: Who can I message regarding financial matters in HZ. A: You can message fin@hz.nl
Q: Where can I find the timetable? A: You can find it on MyHZ where you can add it on the sidebar.
Q: Do I get water bottle. A: Yes
Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ? A: Upload all the files you would like to print to HZ Print, then choose the options you would like then submit your print request.
Q: How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ? A: Information regarding document scanning available Here.
Q: How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ webshop? A: Information regarding document scanning available Here.
Q: Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID? A: Information regarding COVID available Here.
Q: How can you book a project space in one of the wings? A: You can do that from Here.
Q: What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot? A: You need to use your student ID at the barriers for them to open.
